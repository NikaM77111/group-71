About Dolphins

Dolphins are very intelligence, playfull, and friendly Sea Animals. they swim really fast and usually lives longer than 50 years

Interestinng Facts about Dolphins

  • Smart: Dolphins can learn tricks and solve problems.
  • Breathe Air: They breathe air like humans, not water.
  • Playful: Dolphins love playing with each other
  • Long Lives: Some dolphins live up to 50 years.
