Front-end vs Back-end Development

What is Front-end?

Front-end is the part of a website that you can see. It has text, images, buttons, and colors. It is what users touch and use.

What is Back-end?

Back-end is the part you cannot see. It works in the background. It saves data, checks passwords, and connects to the database.

3 Main Parts of Front-end

  1. HTML – Makes the structure (text, images, buttons).
  2. CSS – Makes it look nice (colors, layout, design).
  3. JavaScript – Makes it move or
