About Prison Break

Plot Summary

Prison Break is a suspenseful drama about Michael Scofield, a brilliant structural engineer who devises an elaborate plan to break his innocent brother, Lincoln Burrows, out of prison after he is sentenced to death.

Creator

Paul Scheuring

Main Cast

  • Wentworth Miller as Michael Scofield
  • Dominic Purcell as Lincoln Burrows
  • Sarah Wayne Callies as Dr. Sara Tancredi
  • Robert Knepper as Theodore "T-Bag" Bagwell
  • Amaury Nolasco as Fernando Sucre
  • William Fichtner as Alexander Mahone

First Released

2005
